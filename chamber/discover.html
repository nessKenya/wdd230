Discover Mombasa

Last Visited

Mombasa is a Historical rich place that receives thousands of visitors locally as well as Internationally. The high number of tourists as well as scientists poses a great opportunity to ISPs to improve their services as those traveling want to keep tabs with their families.

We live in the social media era where people love sharing what they are experiencing and keeping the memories online. Can you imagine the presence of a reliable ISP, what will that do to those who love fun and love sharing with the world their experiences? There is a market untapped with activities such as Jetskiing, Kayakiing and Surfing among many others.

Cusines is what makes places unique. When you visit a town, you find houses built uniquely, streets decorated in ways that express what the people believe. However, what makes everything special is the taste of food and drinks. There are amazing traditionally designed hotels such as Forodhani and Ambalala, where, you enjoy the local cuisine while feeling the sea breeze and possibly surfing the internet. Can you imagine calling family to show them your newly done on your bucketlist and they find out it is something they have never heard of?

Mombasa is booming with both small scale businesses as well as medium sized businesses. The local government known as the County Government of Mombasa has created enabling environment that helps the SMEs to operate in a good environment. A reliable ISP with proper training will come in handy to these business people as they try to explore more potential markets.

Mombasa Port is the largest port in East and Central Africa. The position coupled with improved means of transport that includes air, train and road makes the movement of goods faster. Unfortunately, one of the headaches of freight and logistics company is ensuring that their entered data is easily synced so that their clients wherever they are, they are aware of the activities happening. This creates a massive opportunity for improved and reliable ISP.

Sports has a large role in any society. Sports can be for merely competition for silverware or it can be an opportunity to unwind from work and other engagement and at the same time it can be used for team building. No matter the type or aim of the sport, one thing remains important, communication. The communication can be News Reporters providing news back to their editors which needs to be done fast. It can also be team leaders reporting the progress of team building. ISPs need to capitalize on this opportunity.

Mombasa Demography

According to the latest government demography and statistics, Mombasa has 610,257 males and 598,046 females. This shows that the number of men is higher than women, which was a little startling. A good percentage of these population are working and raising children. Internet has changed education massively. Today, assignments are done and submitted online.

One of the world's fears is drop in population overtime. However, this hasn't been same application with Mombasa. Mombasa population according to Census has been on the rise. A higher population with many demanding good and reliable needs ISPs to listen and improve on their services. The population clearly is an indicator of untapped opportunity by the ISP.

DESCRIPTION

Our Target Audience consist of students, professionals, techies. Even though we are providing a home for Internet Service Providers within Mombasa, we also target provides the consumers who need web access to their businesses and home use. Additionally, we aim at attracting investors who will invest in resources needed to improve service delivery.

SCENARIOS

  • The students need affordable internet that is reliable for their online classes, assignments as well as entertainment in their free time. This information will be available for them.
  • The Tech local businesses such as software companies, laptop and phone repairs as well as those selling electronics need internet to keep their customers engaged as they solve their tech-related challenges.
  • The ISP businesses want to expand their businesses to reach new areas in Mombasa, where can they get trusted investors?
  • Investors interested in putting their money towards improving local internet service providers needing to know where their money has potential to grow and possibly invite bigger investors.
  • Consumers having questions on the internet service provider prices, reliability, reviews from current customers and uptime.
  • Hotels and other small businesses needing a reliable internet service provider for their customers as a way of them attracting more customers to their sercices.
